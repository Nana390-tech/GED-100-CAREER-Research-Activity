📚 Research the Jobs

LEVEL 1 ▼
Mission: Visit the three websites below. Read about each job. Then write your notes in the tables. Write at least 100 words in each box.
⚠️

Do NOT copy and paste! After you read the information on the websites, close the page and write the information in your own words. This is called paraphrasing. Copying text directly from a website is academic dishonesty and will not be accepted.

🔗

Synthesise your research: Do not use only one website. Read 2 or 3 websites and then combine the ideas together in your own paragraph. For example: if CareerExplorer says the job is rewarding and Prospects says it is well paid, write both ideas together in your own sentence.

👩‍🏫 Kindergarten Teacher – My Research Notes
📋Job Description
What does this person do?

✍️ Tips

  • Start: A kindergarten teacher is someone who…
  • Who do they work with? What age?
  • What do they do every day?
  • Do they work with parents or other teachers?
  • Use at least 2 websites
Words: 0 / 100 min
⭐Skills Needed
What skills does this job need?

✍️ Tips

  • Name at least 5 different skills
  • For each skill, explain why it is important
  • Use: also, in addition, furthermore
  • Think: personal, communication, practical skills
Words: 0 / 100 min
👍Advantages
What is good about this job?

✍️ Tips

  • List at least 3–4 advantages
  • Think: salary, satisfaction, hours, opportunities
  • Use: One advantage is… / Another benefit is…
  • Use more than one website
Words: 0 / 100 min
👎Disadvantages
What is difficult?

✍️ Tips

  • List at least 3–4 disadvantages
  • Think: stress, hours, salary, physical demands
  • Use: One disadvantage is… / However,…
  • Be honest — every job has challenges!
